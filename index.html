Collatz Conjecture Calculator

The Collatz Conjecture is an unsolved math problem that asks if two arithmetic sequences will eventually turn all positive integers to one.

This calculator will determine the number of steps any positive number will take to reach 1.

The rules are as follows, Odd number: multiply by 3, add 1. Even: divide by 2.

When you enter a positive integer, it will show the amount of steps and the sequence of numbers taken to 1.
