Collatz Conjecture Calculator

The Collatz Conjecture, or 3n+1 is a math problem that asks if two operations will eventually turn all positive integers to 1.

This calculator will determine the amount of steps any positive number will take to reach 1.

The rules are as follows, Odd number: multiply by 3, add 1. Even: divide by 2.

When you enter a positive integer, it will show the amount of steps and the sequence of numbers taken to 1.
